Keys and the internet

The metaphor of keys offers the ability to break down the barriers of locks. Keys on the internet are much like the physical as they are a way of opening content. Individuals with accounts to particular sites on the internet have keys (passwords) for these locks, allowing them to access personal content which is otherwise protected from unintended users.

On occasions keys are poorly made, resulting in intruders emulating or picking the lock and accessing personal content behind these doors.
